A R N A V

SCOIT organises “Arnav”- annual techno-management branch event in order to welcome first year students as well as develop their technical and managerial acumen. Students are subjected three days of rigorous procedure of varied events which allow them to hone their skills and moreover, understand the essence of what Information Technology is all about.
SCOIT not only caters to needs of first year students but also aims to hone organising and managerial skills of the other students who conceptualise and coordinate the events.
